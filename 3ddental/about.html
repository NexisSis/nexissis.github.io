About
Dr. Angela McMullin
Is our most recent member of the team. Her experience includes two years as the lead general dentist at a corporate practice in South East Florida. Prior to her corporate practice experience, Dr. McMullin spent two years in clinical training at the Kornberg School of Dentistry in Philadelphia.
Dr. McMullin is passionate about all aspects of health care; in particular as it pertains to oral health and the impact it has on overall wellness. Dr. McMullin is an enthusiastic integrator of technology within her practice and she is an avid student; pursuing a great many passions through continuing education courses and certification programs. Outside of her professional activities
  • Dr. McMullin enjoys the beach, playing competitive tennis (5.0+) and spending time with family in NJ, where she was born and raised.
  • She belongs to SFDA, ADA and the AAFE organizations. Dr. Angela McMullin graduated from The Ohio State University with a B.A degree in Psychology and a minor in Life Sciences.
  • She earned her Master's of Biomedical Science from the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New J
ersey following which Dr. McMullin attended Temple University, Kornberg School of Dentistry where she received her D.M.D (Doctor of Dental Medicine).
Dr. Sydney Sher Segall
Is one of the leading doctors of General and Cosmetic dentistry in the South Florida region. She was born and raised in Miami and is thrilled to be serving her home community. She attended the University of Pennsylvania where she received her Bachelor's degree and graduated at the top of her class with Magna Cum Laude status. Dr. Sher then returned home to South Florida to obtain her dental degree from Nova Southeastern University. Dr. Sher has a passion for precision and perfection as well as developing great relationships with her adored patients. Her large following enjoys her gentle touch, vibrant personality, and vast knowledge of the field of dentistry.
  • Her skills include fillings, veneers, crowns, bridges, implants, root canals, extractions, dentures, Invisalign, teeth whitening, Botox and Juvederm.
  • Her devotion and love for her patients
  • Her extraordinary skills are the reason for her very busy practice. Outside of the office, Dr. Sher enjoys spending time with her husband and baby boy and as well as the rest of her family and friends.
State of the art Panoramic X-Ray Machine
Our digital panoramic X-ray machine captures a highly detailed image of your entire mouth and clearly shows your teeth, sinuses, jaw joints and the bone levels surrounding your teeth. Digital X-rays make diagnosis and treatment planning far more precise and efficient.

Prosthodontic Dentistry of South Florida
Prosthodontic Dentistry of South Florida is conveniently located in the Biscayne Bank Building in Coconut Grove/Miami, Florida. Our Coconut Grove dentists, Dr. Sharp, Dr Segall, Dr Ravazzi, Dr Ribeiro, Dr. Pasciuta, Dr. Sher and their staff are able to provide specialized, general and cosmetic dentistry, including crowns, bridges, veneers, root canal treatments, pediatric dentistry, Invisalign and more. We have prosthodontists, maxillo oral facial prosthodontists, a periodontist, general dentists, an oral surgeon, an endodontist, and a full hygienist department to fulfill all your needs as patients.
