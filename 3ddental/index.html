Cosmetic dentistry
Your facial appearance is one of your most important assets. Cosmetic Dentistry has developed long lasting cosmetic methods to ensure that your teeth stay straight, clean and strong. At 3D Dental we can whiten your teeth, strengthen your teeth and cover up all those unsightly cracks and holes that make you want to keep your mouth shut.
Veneers & Crowns
If you have any damaged or cracked teeth our veneers and crown technology may be just what you are looking for. Both add strength to your teeth and added support to your jaw, as well of course improving your facial appearance and your smile.
Invisible braces
Have you ever thought that you will never get your teeth straightened because you don’t want to be mocked for months on end with metal braces in your mouth? That’s all changed, as now there are braces like Invisalign available to both adults and children to fix that crooked smile, invisibly!
Dental implants
Dental implants look and feel like your own teeth. And because they are designed to fuse with bone, they become permanent
Teeth whitening
The dream of having a nice sparkling, white smile can now be met by our cutting edge teeth whitening treatments that will bring back your confidence and your smile in no time at all.
Dental anxiety
You don’t need to feel anxious when you visit 3D Dental as we have the most advanced relaxation treatments available to ensure your dental treatment goes quickly and smoothly. You will leave wondering what all the fuss was all about.
Injectables
Injectables There is increasingly a “mix-and-match” approach when it comes to injectables to fill in sagging skin and wrinkles. At 3D Dental we use the best tried and tested products that keep your skin smooth and fresh.
Fillers
As you age, sagging skin on your face and more wrinkles can’t be stopped, but these sad features can be treated by using modern dermal fillers that fill in the wrinkles and the sags giving you a fuller, shapelier face.
